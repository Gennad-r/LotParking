About the System

Effective and convenient solution

LOT Group offers an automated car park management system

LOT Parking system is a modern solution and is intended for the automated control of vehicle entry and exit and the automation of payments for parking services.

Parking entry stand

Parking entry stand is intended to control the cars driving to the paid car park. The entry stand delivers a contactless card for further payment for parking services according to current tariffs. It provides work with VIP cards. The entry stand is installed in the car park entrance area in front of the barrier.

Parking exit stand

Parking exit stand is intended to control the cars driving out from the paid car park. The exit stand accepts contactless cards and intended for control of payment for parking services. It provides work with VIP cards. It is installed in the car park exit area in front of the barrier.

Inductive loop vehicle detector

The inductive loop vehicle detector is designed to remotely detect the presence of a car in the coverage area of ​​the parking stands. The principle of its operation is based on determining the mass of the vehicle in the area of ​​the sensor.

Payment terminal

Payment terminal is intended for the parking service payment without cashier’s assistance. The payment terminal is installed on the territory of the car park in the places suitable for the drivers.

Barrier

Barriers provide control of the vehicle entry and exit of cars on the territory of the car park. The choice of the model is determined by the intensity of its use.

LED traffic light

The LED traffic light informs the driver about the possibility of entry and exit from the car park and has well distinguishable LED indicators of two colours.

Card reader

A desktop reader is designed to prepare and enter contactless cards into the system. The reader is installed on the Automated workplace cashier’s and administrator’s AWP.

Free parking spaces display

The free parking spaces display is a parking information display that shows the number of available parking spaces.

Height sensor

The use of the height sensor allows to extend the possibilities of the car park management system. LOT Parking allows applying different types of tariffs depending on the height of the vehicle (cars, trucks).

IP SIP Intercom for communication with the operator

Intercom provides the driver with a two-way voice communication with the system operator on duty in the event of emergency situations.

System Server

System server is intended for database forming, storing and administering with all parameters and statistic data about car park operation. The database server is a computer with installed dedicated software.

AWP

The automated workplace of the administrator, cashier and operator is a personal computer intended for the direct work of users of systems. It is designed for setting the parameters of the car park management system, maintenance, report configuring and payment control.

Dedicated software

Dedicated software developed by LOT Group specialists supervises and administers the work of all devices of the automated car park management system. Created, taking into account the requirements and requests of our customers (parking owners), our software allows administering car park management systems of any complexity.

Discount Validator

Discount validator is another tool that provides flexible management of tariff plans. Discount validator allows to apply special discounts for car park users at reception of the hotels, in the stores of shopping centres, exhibition complexes, etc.) or use different tariff plans for different zones of one car park.

How LOT Parking works

1. The sensor in the area in front of the entry stand detects the vehicle approach to the barrier.
2. The driver presses the button on the entry stand and receives a parking card.
3. The driver enters the car park. The barrier closes. The system starts counting the time of parking.
4. Before leaving the car park the driver pays for parking services depending on the type of tariff in the payment terminal.
5. The sensor in the area in front of the exit stand detects the vehicle approach to the barrier, the driver gives the paid parking card, the barrier opens.
6. The sensor records the vehicle’s departure from the car park, the barrier closes.

Tariff system

LOT Parking system provides a flexible tariff and discount systems. Depending on the customer’s requirements the system allows to choose the necessary tariff and discounts. Tariff plans can be divided into time periods of different duration with the possibility to set discounts and introduce various loyalty programs for customers.

The system provides the possibility of temporary entry and exit of vehicles.
